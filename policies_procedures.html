Environment, Health, and Safety Policy Statement

Our School is committed to providing a safe and healthy environment for all students, staff, and visitors. We recognize that the well-being of our school community is essential to achieving our educational mission.

Policy Statement

Our school is committed to:

  • Complying with all applicable ADEK, MOE, and UAE health, safety, and environmental regulations
  • Preventing injuries, illnesses, and environmental impacts through proactive risk management
  • Continuously improving our EHS performance through regular reviews and updates
  • Providing adequate resources to implement and maintain effective EHS programs
  • Promoting a culture of safety awareness and responsibility among all members of our school community

Responsibilities

School Leadership:

  • Establish and maintain an effective EHS management system
  • Provide necessary resources for EHS programs
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Lead by example in promoting safety culture

Staff and Faculty:

  • Follow all EHS policies and procedures
  • Report hazards, incidents, and near misses promptly
  • Participate in EHS training and initiatives
  • Incorporate safety education into teaching where appropriate

Students:

  • Follow safety rules and instructions
  • Report unsafe conditions to teachers or staff
  • Participate in safety drills and training
  • Contribute to maintaining a clean and safe environment

Implementation

This policy will be implemented through:

  • Regular risk assessments and safety inspections
  • Comprehensive EHS training programs
  • Effective incident reporting and investigation procedures
  • Emergency preparedness and response planning
  • Regular review and continuous improvement of EHS performance

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure its continued relevance and effectiveness.

Approved by: School Principal

Date: March 15, 2025
