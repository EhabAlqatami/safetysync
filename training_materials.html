Current Module: Evacuation Procedures

Module Content

This module covers the proper procedures for evacuating a building during a fire emergency. You will learn about evacuation routes, assembly points, and how to assist others during an evacuation.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the importance of evacuation plans
  • Identify primary and secondary evacuation routes
  • Learn how to assist others during an evacuation
  • Understand the role of fire wardens
