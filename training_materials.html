Current Module: Evacuation Procedures

Module Content

This module covers the proper procedures for evacuating a building during a fire emergency. You will learn about evacuation routes, assembly points, and how to assist others during an evacuation.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the importance of evacuation plans
  • Identify primary and secondary evacuation routes
  • Learn how to assist others during an evacuation
  • Understand the role of fire wardens

Module Quiz

  • A. Call the fire department
  • B. Gather your belongings
  • C. Evacuate immediately using the nearest exit
  • D. Look for the source of the fire
2. Where should you go after evacuating the building?
  • A. To your car
  • B. To the designated assembly point
  • C. Back inside when the alarm stops
  • D. Home
3. What should you do if your primary evacuation route is blocked?
  • A. Wait for help
  • B. Try to clear the blockage
  • C. Use the secondary evacuation route
  • D. Return to your workspace
