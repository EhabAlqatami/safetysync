Risk Details - Science Laboratory Chemical Storage

Risk Information

Location: Science Laboratories
Department: Teaching Staff
Risk Level: High Risk
Last Review: April 10, 2025
Responsible Person: Ahmed Hassan

Risk Description

Improper storage of chemicals in the science laboratory poses a risk of chemical spills, reactions, and exposure. Current storage cabinets are not properly labeled, and some incompatible chemicals are stored in close proximity. There is also inadequate ventilation in the chemical storage area.

Control Measures

Reorganize chemical storage according to compatibility
In Progress
Separate chemicals by hazard class and ensure incompatible chemicals are stored separately. Implement color-coded labeling system.
Install proper ventilation system
Pending
Install extraction fans and ensure adequate air exchange in the chemical storage area.
Provide chemical spill kits
Completed
Place appropriate spill kits in the laboratory and storage areas. Train staff on proper use.
Update chemical inventory system
In Progress
Implement digital inventory system to track chemicals, their quantities, locations, and expiration dates.
